ENEL300 Course Talks

Image source

ENEL300 brought in lots of guest speakers that gave talks about things like entrepreneurship, APEGA, empathy, etc. One of the talks I thought worked well was about interviews and jobs.

They spoke about tenacity in job searches, and explained their journey in finding the right job. It is important to not get discouraged when you don't get the right fit.

However, I think the most important message I took away was that interviews are bidirectional. Rather than trying to impress an interviewer, you are there to learn more about the company and people to see if you can work together.
