ENEL300 Course

Image source

ENEL300 focused on Scrum, one of the popular frameworks for implementing agile development. This means things like product backlog, sprint backlog, daily standups, burndown charts, user personas, etc.

The most challenging part of the Scrum process for me is keeping up daily maintenance work. For example, organizing daily standups, filling in data on the burndown chart, etc. I tend to forget a couple days when things get busy. However, I find that using a to do list app helps with staying on track.

This course helped me learn how to be adaptable and agile in the design process, and got me familiar with Scrum.
